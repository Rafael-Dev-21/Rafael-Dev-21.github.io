## infinito

$$1\div 0=\infy$$
$$2\div 0=\infy$$
$$2=1$$
$$1+\infy=\infy, \infy - \infy = 1$$


## NaN (not a number)
$$0\div 0=NaN$$
$$1^\infy =NaN$$

## imaginários e complexos
$$\sqrt{-1}=i$$
$$(1+1i)^2=0$$
