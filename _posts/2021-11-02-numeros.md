## infinito

$$1\div 0=\infty$$
$$2\div 0=\infty$$
$$2=1$$
$$1+\infty=\infty, \infty - \infty = 1$$

## NaN (not a number)
$$0\div 0=NaN$$
$$1^\infty =NaN$$

## imaginários e complexos
$$\sqrt{-1}=i$$
$$(1+1i)^2=0$$
